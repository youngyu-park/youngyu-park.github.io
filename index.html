About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Linguistics at UT Austin. I earned my B.A. in English Language and Literature and my M.A. in English Linguistics from Kyung Hee University, Seoul, under the supervision of Dr. Jong-Bok Kim. I am currently working with Dr. John Beavers, focusing on the syntax-semantics interface and how pragmatic factors are mapped onto it. I am particularly interested in linguistic constructions, including clefts and ellipsis phenomena, as well as phenomena related to questions and negation.
