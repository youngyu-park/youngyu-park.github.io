Research Interests

My current research focuses on the syntax-semantics interfaces within the field of syntax, as well as how pragmatic factors are mapped onto the SYN-SEM interface.
My specific interest lies in exploring diverse linguistic constructions, including cleft constructions and ellipsis phenomena. Through this linguistic exploration, my goal is to gain a deeper understanding of how language communicates meaning through its structures.
